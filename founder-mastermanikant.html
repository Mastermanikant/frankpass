Master Manikant

Founder & Developer of FrankPass

Hello! I am Master Manikant, the original Founder and Developer of FrankPass. If you are searching for the person who created this futuristic security tool, you've found me. I built FrankPass because I was tired of being a prisoner to my own security and the risks of centralized password vaults.

I wanted a tool that felt like a superpower—a way to generate complex, proof-hard passwords on the fly using nothing but my own personal logic. No database, no accounts, and no monthly fees for the core web tool.

FrankPass is my contribution to digital sovereignty. I believe that core security should be a human right, which is why the FrankPass website and web apps will stay entirely free for life.

To support my work, pay my bills, and continue helping humanity through tech, I plan to launch browser extensions on a paid model. If you can't afford it, just reach out—I'm here to support the community.
