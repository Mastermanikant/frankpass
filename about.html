Our Mission

FrankPass was born out of a simple realization: the modern internet has a "mental load" problem. We are forced to remember dozens of complex passwords, or trust centralized "vaults" that can be hacked, leaked, or locked.

Our mission is to give you mental peace through mathematical certainty. FrankPass is not a storage system; it's a concept. It's the bridge between a simple phrase you'll never forget and an unbreakable password that no supercomputer can guess.

The Zero-Knowledge Philosophy

We don't want your data. In fact, we've designed FrankPass so that it's impossible for us to have it. There are no databases, no user accounts, and no cloud syncing.

Your passwords are generated locally in your browser and destroyed the moment you close the tab. You are the only person in the world who holds the key to your digital life.

Sustainability & Future

I want to make FrankPass free for the world, but I also have bills to pay and a vision to grow and help humanity. Here is my promise: The FrankPass website and web apps will always be entirely free, forever.

To sustain the project, I will eventually launch premium browser extensions with paid plans. However, if anyone is truly unable to pay the fee, you can contact me. I am usually busy, so please allow 7-10 days for a response, but I will do my best to support you.
