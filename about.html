What is FrankPass?

FrankPass is a free, open, and stateless password generator. Unlike traditional password managers that store your passwords in a cloud database, FrankPass does not save anything, anywhere, ever.

Instead, it uses advanced mathematics (cryptographic hashing) to calculate your password every time you need it. Think of it like a calculator: if you type 5 × 5, you always get 25. The calculator doesn't "remember" 25 — the math just produces it. FrankPass works the exact same way with your passwords.

This means there is no database to hack, no vault to breach, and no account to lock you out of. You are the only person who controls your security.

Our Mission

The internet has a "mental load" problem. Every website wants a unique, complex password. The average person has over 100 online accounts. Remembering all those passwords is impossible, so most people either:

  • Reuse the same simple password everywhere (dangerous), or
  • Trust a cloud-based vault to store them (risky if it gets hacked).

Our mission is to give you mental peace through a third option — mathematical certainty. Remember just one personal phrase, and FrankPass generates a unique, unbreakable password for every website you use.

The Zero-Knowledge Promise

We don't want your data. In fact, we have built FrankPass so that it is impossible for us to have it:

  • No databases — We have zero servers storing any user information.
  • No user accounts — You never sign up or log in.
  • No cloud syncing — Everything happens locally in your browser.
  • No tracking or analytics — We don't know who you are or what you do.

Your passwords are generated on your device and destroyed the moment you close the tab. You are the only person in the world who has the key to your digital life.
