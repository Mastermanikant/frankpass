Master Manikant

Founder & Developer of FrankPass

Hello! I am Master Manikant, and I am the original Founder and Developer of FrankPass. I built this tool because I was tired of being a prisoner to my own security. Like many of you, I worried about password managers being breached and the mental load of creating "random but memorable" passwords.

I wanted a tool that felt like a superpower—a way to generate complex, hacker-proof passwords on the fly using nothing but my own personal logic. No database required. No monthly fees. No "Forgot Password" loops.

FrankPass is my contribution to the world of privacy and digital sovereignty. I believe that core security should be accessible to everyone, which is why the website and web apps will stay entirely free for life.

To support my work, pay my bills, and continue helping humanity through tech, I plan to launch browser extensions on a paid model. If you can't afford it, my door is always open—just reach out. (Though I'm often busy, expect a 7-10 day response time!)
