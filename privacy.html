FrankPass

Your data stays with you. Period.

1. No Data Collection

FrankPass is a stateless application. We do not have a database. We do not use tracking cookies. We do not log your generated passwords, usernames, or secret keys.

Every calculation happens locally in your browser's memory and is destroyed immediately after use.

2. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Because the generation logic is 100% client-side, we have "Zero Knowledge" of what you generate. Even if our servers (provided by Vercel) were compromised, there is no user data for a hacker to steal.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Vercel: For hosting the static website files. They may collect basic server logs (IP address, browser type) which are standard for all web hosting but unrelated to your actual passwords.
  • Ionicons: To provide the beautiful icons you see on the site.

4. Your Responsibilities

Since we do not store your Secret Key, we cannot recover it for you. It is your sole responsibility to remember your key. If you forget it, your generated passwords cannot be recovered.

5. Terms of Service

By using FrankPass, you agree that you are using this tool at your own risk. While we use industry-standard cryptography, security is a shared responsibility. We recommend using the "Mental Tweak" method described in our guide for maximum security.
