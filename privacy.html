Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Zero Knowledge & Stateless Architecture

FrankPass is built on the fundamental principle that we do not want your data. Because of our deterministic stateless architecture:

  • We do not store your passwords.
  • We do not save your Secret Key.
  • We do not have a user database.

2. How Your Data is Handled

All cryptographic processing (PBKDF2, HMAC-SHA512) happens inside the memory of your own browser or application window. When you close the tab or the application, all data is instantly destroyed. Nothing is ever sent to or stored on our servers.

3. Serverless API (The Pepper Layer)

If the online version uses our Serverless API to generate the cryptographic pepper, the data is sent over a secure HTTPS connection. The API holds the data in volatile memory purely for the millisecond required to compute the hash. It is then discarded immediately. Logs are disabled.

4. Cookies and Tracking

FrankPass does not use tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or any third-party behavioral monitoring tools.

5. Content from Extensions

Our Chrome Extension requires "activeTab" and "scripting" permissions solely to inject the generated password into your currently active login box. It does not monitor, read, or export data from other tabs or websites.

Contact

If you have questions about our zero-data model, contact us at: contact@frankpass.com
