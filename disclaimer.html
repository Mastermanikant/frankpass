Security & Usage Disclaimer

1. No Guarantee of Absolute Security

Security is a process, not a product. While FrankPass utilizes robust cryptographic algorithms (HMAC-SHA512 + PBKDF2), no system is 100% infallible. Your security is also dependent on the security of your operating system, browser, and hardware.

2. Zero Recovery Disclaimer

FrankPass is strictly stateless. We do not store any part of your password recipe. If you forget your "Secret Personal Key" or your "Variant Number", there is mathematically zero possibility for us to recover your password. Use the "Recipe System" documented in our guide to manage your data safely.

3. Ethical Use

FrankPass is intended for personal and professional account security. We do not condone its use for any illegal activities. Users are responsible for complying with local laws regarding the use of encryption and security tools.

4. Third-Party Risks

FrankPass protects the generation of your password. It does not protect you against keyloggers, screen recording malware, or phishing attacks on external websites. Always ensure you are on the legitimate website (https://frankpass.com) before entering keys.
